# 'Work For Fun'

  • LookingBus

    2018.12
    • LookingBus is an orientation system for the blind that uses proprietary hardware to facilitate communication between blind pedestrians and bus drivers. The system is designed to assist with the challenges of finding bus stops, boarding the correct bus, and exiting at the correct destination. LookingBus aims to enable individuals with disabilities to fully use public transportation systems and provide them with the confidence to overcome the challenges they face on a day to day basis. The ability to travel independently, especially by way of public transportation, allows blind/visually impaired individuals to be physically active, socially engaged, and economically productive. We are now running our alpha testing with Detroit Metroplolitan Airpolrt Transit for our Android product.
